What we fund

Tools, books, boots, certification fees, semester transcripts, deposits at community college, apprenticeship startup costs — $200 to $500 a year, typical. Higher tiers for 4-year nursing at UIC or the University of Chicago.

We disburse to schools and employers, never to individuals. If a kid needs a $400 set of hand tools to start her apprenticeship Monday, the check goes to the shop on Friday.

Who's eligible

Any senior at one of the twelve qualifying high schools below — or homeschooled in the same area — pursuing a career in advanced manufacturing, health sciences, or the building trades.

No essay. No ethnicity question. No citizenship question. Required: legal name, address, ZIP, transcript, FAFSA, and a career objective. [TK]
