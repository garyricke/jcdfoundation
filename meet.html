Who we fund · Three sectors

The careers our scholarships go into.

We don't fund essay-writing or four-year liberal arts. We fund skilled vocations the Fox Valley actually hires for — and where a small check at the right moment removes a real obstacle.

01 · Advanced Manufacturing

Build the things the region runs on.

Machinists · CNC · welders · industrial tech.
Specific roles refined with Valley Industrial Association.
02 · Health Sciences

The clinicians your community needs.

Radiology tech · dental hygiene · RN.
Higher-tier awards available for 4-year nursing at UIC / U of Chicago.
03 · Building Trades

The trades that build the buildings.

Electrician · HVAC · pipefitter · operating engineer.
Apprenticeship-track. Tools, boots, books — not tuition.
The geography · 12 qualifying high schools

The Dunham Quadrant.

JCD funds students from the high schools bounded by Illinois Routes 38, 34, 59, and 47. A loose square around Aurora — the area John C. Dunham called home.
