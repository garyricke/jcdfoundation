This brand guide establishes **JCD Foundation** as a specialized, high-impact offshoot of the Dunham Foundation. While the parent organization focuses on broad regional architecture and systemic capacity building, JCD is the **"Applied Legacy"**—focusing specifically on the individual's path through vocational mastery in manufacturing, health sciences, and the trades.

## ---

**1\. Brand Identity & Philosophy**

### **The Mission**

To catalyze individual prosperity by providing "Just-in-Time" (JIT) educational support for high-demand, skilled vocations. JCD Foundation transforms the industrial precision of John Dunham into a streamlined engine for career mobility.

### **The Core Pillars (The "JCD Standard")**

* **Industrial Precision:** We value technical excellence and the "Just-in-Time" philosophy—delivering resources exactly when and where a student needs them to succeed.  
* **Professional Etiquette:** Following John Dunham’s personal creed, we emphasize promptness, self-presentation, and confidentiality as the bedrock of a professional life.  
* **Wise Gifting:** Our scholarships are not passive charity; they are strategic investments in the "human infrastructure" of the Fox Valley.

## ---

**2\. Strategic Focus Areas**

JCD Foundation differentiates itself by narrowing its scope to three high-impact sectors:

* **Advanced Manufacturing:** Machine tool technology, welding, and logistical innovation.  
* **Health Sciences:** Nursing, diagnostic imaging (MRI/Radiation Therapy), and surgical technology.  
* **Building Trades:** Skilled vocations that maintain and grow the physical community.

## ---

**3\. Visual Identity & Tone**

### **The Visual Aesthetic: "Modern Industrial"**

* **Logo Concept:** A clean, geometric monogram of "JCD" that evokes steel shelving or architectural blueprints, nodding to Equipto’s legacy.  
* **Color Palette:**  
  * **Primary:** *Equipto Blue* (A deep, industrial navy) and *Steel Gray* (representing manufacturing).  
  * **Secondary:** *Pulse Teal* (representing health sciences) and *Safety Orange* (representing building trades/action).  
* **Typography:** Bold, clean sans-serif fonts (e.g., Roboto or Montserrat) to reflect efficiency and modernity.

### **Brand Voice**

* **Direct & Efficient:** Avoid "philanthropic fluff." Use clear, actionable language.  
* **Mentorship-Oriented:** Speak like a master craftsman guiding an apprentice.  
* **Grounded:** Maintain a deep connection to the Aurora/Fox Valley geography.

## ---

**4\. Target Audience & Eligibility**

### **Geographic Bounding**

The brand remains fiercely place-based, serving the "Dunham Quadrant" bounded by Illinois State Routes 38, 34, 59, and 47\.

### **Priority High Schools**

JCD Foundation specifically targets graduates from:

* **Public Districts:** East Aurora, West Aurora, Oswego East, Waubonsie Valley, Metea Valley, Kaneland, Batavia, and Geneva.  
* **Private Academies:** Marmion Academy, Rosary, Aurora Central Catholic, and Aurora Christian.

## ---

**5\. The User Experience (The "KC Model")**

Following the **Great Jobs KC** model, the JCD experience must be frictionless:

* **The Portal:** A "One-Stop" digital interface for applications and progress tracking, integrated with the GivingData philosophy of real-time communication.  
* **Video Integration:** The brand will use high-impact interview videos from partners like **Aurora University**, **Waubonsee**, and **Trade Unions** to demystify the path from classroom to career.
