Section 01 · Logo

The mark, on light and dark

jcd-logo-dark.svg · use on white / ice-gray
jcd-logo-white.svg · use on navy / charcoal

Clear space

Keep an "X" of breathing room equal to the height of the mark's tallest letterform on all four sides. Nothing — no rule, photo, copy, or competing logo — enters this zone.

Minimum size

For business cards and footers, the mark holds down to ~24px / 0.25" tall. Below that, use the wordmark only or substitute the upcoming small-size stencil monogram.

Dear [Name],

On behalf of JCD Foundation — a vocational scholarship initiative for students in the Fox Valley — I'd like to invite you for a brief conversation. We're talking with educators, employers, and apprenticeship leads across Advanced Manufacturing, Health Sciences, and the Building Trades to make sure the foundation we're building actually fits the careers our students are walking into.

The ask is small: roughly 30 minutes, in person or by phone, your call. You set the agenda. We mostly listen. The transcript becomes raw material for the public site we'll launch once our 501(c)(3) determination lands.

You can learn more at jcdfoundation.org/meet — or just reply to this letter and we'll find a time that works.

Thank you for the work you do for our kids.

Bob Vaughan
Founder, JCD Foundation
